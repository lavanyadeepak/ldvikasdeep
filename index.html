What is this?

A small gesture with a big heart

Every day, talented home entrepreneurs share what they make � homemade pickles, tailored clothes, handcrafted jewellery, tiffin services � in messaging groups, relying on word of mouth alone.

They deserve more. So I volunteer my time to design and build each of them a clean, beautiful, professional webpage � completely free. No catch. No charge.

It's my small way of saying: your work matters, and the world should see it.

💬

How it works

Simple as a WhatsApp message

No technical knowledge needed. You just share what you do � I'll handle the rest.

01

02
