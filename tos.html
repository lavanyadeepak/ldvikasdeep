✦ ◈ ✦ ◈ ✦

Our Little Agreement 🪔

A simple, honest understanding between us — so we can work together joyfully, with no surprises on either side.

Version 1.0  ·  Effective June 2025

LD VikasDeep is a volunteer initiative by Lavanya Deepak — a free service that designs and builds web pages for home-based small businesses in our WhatsApp community. This is offered as an act of community care, with no fees, no contracts, and no hidden obligations.

This document simply sets out what you can expect from me — and what I kindly ask in return. It is written in plain language, with warmth, because that is the spirit in which this service is offered.

Section 01

What LD VikasDeep Offers

As part of this free community initiative, I will design and publish one web page for your home-based business. This includes:

  • A mobile-friendly webpage showcasing your products or services, contact details, and photos you provide.
  • Up to 2 rounds of revisions after the initial design — to get the details right.
  • A shareable link you can use on WhatsApp, visiting cards, and social media.
  • Basic updates (e.g. phone number change, new product) up to once every 3 months, on a best-effort basis.
🪔 This is a gift of time and skill. Turnaround time is typically 5–10 days from when you share your details, depending on my availability. I will keep you updated.
Section 02

Revisions — Keeping It Kind

I genuinely want your page to feel right for you. At the same time, this is a volunteer service and my time is finite. Here is how revisions work:

  • Round 1: After I share the first draft, please review carefully and send all your feedback together in one message.
  • Round 2: One more round of refinements based on your feedback.
  • Beyond 2 rounds: Additional changes beyond this are at my discretion. I may kindly decline if the scope keeps expanding.

Please share clear, consolidated feedback — sending changes one by one over many messages makes the process difficult for both of us. A little patience goes a long way. 🙏

Section 03

Your Content, Your Responsibility

You are responsible for the content (text, photos, prices, claims) that appears on your page. By sharing content with me, you confirm that:

  • It is accurate and honest — no misleading claims about your products or services.
  • You own or have the right to use it — photos, logos, and other materials are yours to share.
  • It does not promote anything illegal, harmful, or offensive.

I reserve the right to decline or remove content that I find inappropriate, misleading, or inconsistent with the community spirit of this initiative.

Section 04

For Personal Business Use Only

The page I build for you is meant to support your personal home-based business — the one you told me about. Please do not:

  • Sell or transfer the page to another person or business.
  • Use it to promote a different business without informing me first.
  • Claim the design as your own creation or pass it off as self-made work.
  • Use it for anything that violates Indian law or harms others in the community.
This page is built as an act of community service. It is not a commercial product and should not be treated as one.
Section 05

A Small Credit — The #GivingBack Footer

Each page includes a small #GivingBack footer at the bottom — acknowledging that it was built as part of this community initiative and linking back to my profile page.

I kindly ask that this footer remain on your page as long as it is live. It does not take anything away from your business — in fact, it adds a story of community warmth to your page. 🪔

If you have a specific reason to request its removal, please speak to me directly. I am always open to a kind conversation.

Section 06

Who Owns What

  • Your content is yours. All text, photos, and business information you provide remain your property.
  • The design is mine. The layout, code, and visual design created by me remain my intellectual work. I may use screenshots of your page in my portfolio to showcase this initiative (without sharing private contact information).
  • No one owns the page exclusively. I host and maintain it as a volunteer. I may discontinue hosting with reasonable notice (at least 30 days) if circumstances require.
Section 07

When a Page May Be Taken Down

I reserve the right to take down a page — with or without prior notice — in the following situations:

  • The content has been found to be false, harmful, or illegal.
  • The terms of this agreement have been violated.
  • I am unable to continue hosting due to personal or technical reasons.
  • The business has closed or changed significantly without informing me.

In normal circumstances, I will always try to give at least 30 days' notice before taking a page offline, so you can make alternative arrangements.

Section 08

Honest Limitations

This is a volunteer service offered in good faith. While I will always do my best, please understand:

  • I cannot guarantee that your page will bring a specific number of customers or sales.
  • I cannot guarantee 100% uptime or uninterrupted hosting at all times.
  • I am not responsible for any business loss arising from the page being temporarily unavailable.
In short: I give this freely and wholeheartedly. I ask only for kindness, patience, and fairness in return.
Section 09

Let's Always Talk First

If you ever have a concern, disagreement, or question — please reach out to me directly on WhatsApp before anything else. Most things can be resolved with a simple, honest conversation.

This initiative was built on trust and community spirit. Let's keep it that way. 🙏
